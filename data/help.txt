COMMANDS:

look                        –   show full room description
examine <item>              –   show item description
go <way>                    –   go to the room in (north, east, south, west)
inventory                   -   show all items in inventory
take <item>                 –   take item from the room to your inventory
drop <item>                 –   drop the item from your inventory
give <character> <item>     –   give item to character (needed for quests)
use <item>                  –   use items ability (consumable items are for one time use)
combine <item1> <item2>     –   combine items (crafting mechanic)
talk <character>            –   talk with selected character (use multiple times for all dialogues)
trade <character>           –   show all items character has listed for trade
trade <character> <item>    –   offer character an item to trade (<item> is the item you're giving out)
help                        –   display help instructions (this screen)
quit                        –   quit the game
